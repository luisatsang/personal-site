Luisa Tsang

Experience

Luisa Tsang has over five years of experience in healthcare administration with a focus on data service and analytics, data systems, and quality improvement. Most recently, she served as the Director of Quality Improvement and Data Analytics at OLE Health, the only Federally Qualified Health Center in Napa County. In this capacity, Luisa directed and led multiple organization-wide data analytics initiatives and quality improvement projects that resulted in:

  • recognition by NCQA for Level 3 Patient Centered Medical Home certification for 4 sites;
  • implementation of a robust data analytics infrastructure resulting in the development of over 85 recurring reports and dashboards and an established data processing workflow;
  • 90% of providers achieving Stage 2 Meaningful Use bringing in over $500,000 of incentive payments;
  • development of a robust empanelment process resulting in 99% of all patients properly empaneled;
  • and over $1.3 million of incentive payment from a Medi-Cal health plan for successfully meeting quality goals.
Luisa also led the development of a Quality Improvement Committee and OLE's inaugural Organizational Dashboard. She was a key member in OLE Health's Access Improvement Pilot, development of Strategic Plan metrics, Evidence Based Care Committee, and UDS and OSHPD reporting. As a result, OLE Health has achieved national recognition for high levels of quality performance as a recipient of the Department of Health and Human Services' Health Center Quality Improvement Fund. Luisa is a San Francisco native, received a BA in Neuroscience and Psychology at Smith College, a MPH at Yale University School of Public Health, and completed an Administrative Fellowship Program at Northwell Health System in New York.
